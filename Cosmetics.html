Custom Cosmetics - Personalised Beauty Products for Women

Given Problem
Eliminate Lipstick Wastage
Redefined Problem
A user research was first carried out to understand usage patterns and the lipstick market. The key user patterns identified from the study were:

Women generally do not care about wastage as they either no longer like the shade, lose the lipstick or trend changes.

Lipstick brands would not easily switch to new design of lipstick that eliminates waste as they would have to integrate it across all their suppliers.

Thus, just redesigning the lipstick tube to eliminate wastage would not suffice -> The problem scope had to be redefined.
"Improve the shopping experience by increasing the confidence of the women while purchasing a lipstick or any cosmetic product."

Custom Cosmetics - Get what YOU want

Online Market Place Mockup
Market Place Ecosystem
The customer segment targeted are women between age of 16-26 years since they always feel to be trendy and are ready to experiment with different shades. The team conceptualized a social network kind of webstore with following feature:

• Personalize - make your product; choose from the color of the tube to the shade of the lipstick.

• Share - let other people know what you are making. See and choose from what others are making.

• Earn - earn royalty points if other people use your shade. Redeem it for exciting offers on the store.
