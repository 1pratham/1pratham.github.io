Point of Sale System

About
During summer 2011, I did a one month internship at Vardhaman Technology, Mumbai. It is a integrated embedded systems company specializinf in thin client PCs. My primary role was to design a Point of Sale (POS) system - its external body and internal mounting components: a 10.1 in LCD screen, an Intel Atom based motherboard and a 320 gb Hard Disk
Design
The design of the various components was done mainly considering:
• Ease of use and ideal viewing angles of screen for user
• Placing of the rosens (weldments) for the internal mounting of the components using fasteners
• Design for Manufacturing mainly ease of assembly of the components
