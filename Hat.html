Electronic Hat for Computer Pointer Maneuvering

About
Although the invention of personal computers has made computing facilities available to masses, its usability for physically disabled persons especially quadriplegic patients has been limited. In order to develop a cost effective solution that can improve the computer usability for quadriplegic patients, my two roommates at IIT-GN and I developed an electronic hat for computer mouse pointer maneuvering.
What We Did
The head movements of the user are converted to the direction of mouse motion. Tools used are:
• Cap
• Accelerometer
• Arduino
• USB cable
• Processing
The analog output of accelerometer mounted on the hat is converted to digital by Arduino which is fed to the Processing code that facilitates the mouse movements.
