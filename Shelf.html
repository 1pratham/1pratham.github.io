Linkage Mechanism to Switch Two Book Shelves

About
There are generally four shelves in a library book rack. However, only the middle two shelves are easily accesible and the top and bottom shelves are not. What if the shelves can be interchanged on the go without disturbing the books so that all the shelves are easily accesible when needed?
What We Did
The project was done as part of Kinematics and Dynamics of Machines Lab with two other team members. The main objective we set was to develop a linkage mechanism that facilitates the interchanging of two shelves. Additionally, a prototype was made to demonstrate the working of the linkage mechanism.
